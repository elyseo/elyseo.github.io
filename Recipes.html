Shaping a Sourdough Boule

Shaping is the step most beginners struggle with. This video walks through the technique I use — the same one shown in my method.

🎬 Video: Shaping technique for a round sourdough loaf (boule). Captions and transcript available via the YouTube player.

What to Watch For

Shaping is about building surface tension, not kneading. The dough should feel like you're dragging it across the bench, not pressing it down.

🎯 Key Technique

A light dusting of flour on the bench — too much and the dough won't grip. You want just enough that it slides when you want it to and grips when you need it to.
