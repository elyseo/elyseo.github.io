How to Embed a YouTube Video

A step-by-step guide

Step 1: Find a Video

Search for your favorite video on YouTube and open it.

Step 2: Click Share

Below the video on the right there is a share button, click it.

Step 3: Select Embed

Choose the Embed option.

Step 4: Copy Code

Copy the iframe embed code.

Step 5: Paste into HTML

Pase the code into your HTML file
