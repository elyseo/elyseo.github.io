Hibiscus Plant

The hibiscus plant is one of our best sellers. It is moderetly easy to care for. It thrives under the sun and consistent moisture.

Plumeria Plant

Plumerias love tropical weather. They need 6-8 hours of direct sunlight, however they only need to be watered when their soil is dry.

Bird of Paradise

Birds of paradise love bright direct sunlight for 4-6 hours. They can handle medium sunlight but will grow slower. They love the humidity, and consistent watering.

Pharaoh's Mask Elephant Ears

Pharaoh's Mask is another tropical plant that thrives in high heat and high humidity. Although this plant loves high heat it prefers indirect sunlight. This plant works well in a pot as well as in the ground.
